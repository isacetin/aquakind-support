AquaKind Account Deletion

If you would like to request deletion of your AquaKind account and associated data, please contact us at:

Email: isacetinn@outlook.com

What to include

Please send your request from the email address associated with your account, or include enough information for us to locate your account.

What will be deleted

Upon verification of your request, we will delete your account and associated personal data stored by AquaKind, unless retention is required for legal, security, or fraud-prevention reasons.

Retention

Some limited records may be retained for a short period where required to comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, or enforce our agreements.
